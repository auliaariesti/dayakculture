DAYAK CULTURE

The Dayak tribe is a tribe in Indonesia that inhabits the interior of Kalimantan. Primitive life and far from access to global information are their characteristics.

Love The Places

The traditional house of the Dayak people is called the Betang house or the long house. Namely, the typical Kalimantan traditional house that you can find in areas across Kalimantan, or to be precise in the upstream area of the river which is the center of residence of the Dayak people.
